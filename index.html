Medical Pathway using Generative AI

Problem Definition

India's healthcare sector faces challenges in real-time decision-making, supply chain inefficiencies, and uneven resource distribution.

Objective

Build an AI-powered assistant using Generative AI (Gemini) to provide intelligent, state-wise recommendations for medical resource allocation and infrastructure planning.

1. User Query (Healthcare Staff)

2. NLP Module (Gemini AI)

Assumptions

The implementation of this system assumes access to accurate healthcare datasets, including regional population density, hospital coverage, and existing resource stock. Additionally, it assumes the presence of a minimal cloud infrastructure capable of supporting AI queries, and basic digital literacy among healthcare workers who will interact with the system. Lastly, it is expected that administrative support and policy alignment will be available for real-world integration.

Future Scope

Future enhancements of the system may include regional language support for improved accessibility in rural India. It also opens up possibilities for integration with national healthcare systems such as Aarogya Setu or Ayushman Bharat. Another direction is the development of hospital-specific dashboards that visualize resource metrics and supply chain KPIs. Over time, the model can evolve to provide patient-specific treatment pathways, transforming into a holistic diagnostic and recommendation assistant for front-line medical professionals.
